About Us

Welcome to MedicAI, your trusted companion on the journey to better health. At MedicAI, we strive to providing accurate and reliable medical assessments, empowering individuals to take control of their well-being.

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology to improve the way medical diagnoses are made. With a combination of advanced machine learning algorithms, deep leaning and GenAI, we strive to deliver personalized and insightful healthcare solutions to our users.

How It Works

Our medical diagnosis app is powered by Flask, a powerful Python web framework, both for the frontend and backend. Behind the scenes, our state-of-the-art llama 2 model, meticulously fine-tuned, conducts the diagnosis process with precision and efficiency, making sure not to divulge any peronally identifiable information (PII), protecting user identity.
